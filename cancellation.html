Cancellation Policy

Cancellation terms vary by property. Your specific policy is shown at booking.

Last updated: April 2026 · Applies to all direct bookings through premiumrentals.com

Overview

At Premium Rentals, cancellation policies are set on a per-property basis to reflect each property's demand, seasonality, and owner requirements. Your specific cancellation policy is displayed on the property listing page and confirmed in your booking confirmation email.

By completing your reservation, you acknowledge and agree to the cancellation policy applicable to that specific property. Please review your policy carefully before booking.

📋 Your cancellation policy is always shown before you complete payment. Check your booking confirmation for the exact terms that apply to your reservation.

Policy Tiers

Premium Rentals properties may operate under one of the following cancellation policy tiers:

Flexible
Flexible

Full refund if cancelled at least 24 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 24 hours of check-in are non-refundable. No refund for no-shows.

Moderate
Moderate

Full refund if cancelled at least 5 days before check-in. Cancellations within 5 days receive a 50% refund of the nightly rate. Cleaning fees are non-refundable.

Strict
Strict

50% refund if cancelled at least 7 days before check-in. No refund for cancellations within 7 days of check-in. Cleaning and service fees are non-refundable.

Non-Refundable
Non-Refundable

No refund under any circumstances. These reservations are typically offered at a discounted rate in exchange for the non-refundable nature of the booking.

What's Always Non-Refundable

Regardless of the cancellation policy tier, the following are always non-refundable once a reservation is confirmed:

  • Service fees
  • Cleaning fees (unless the full reservation qualifies for a full refund under a Flexible policy)
  • Any applicable taxes on non-refundable amounts
  • Pet fees collected post-booking

How to Cancel

To request a cancellation, please contact us as soon as possible:

  • Email: info@premiumrentals.com
  • Phone: 208-807-3302 (available 24/7)

Please include your confirmation code and the reason for cancellation. Refunds, where applicable, will be returned to your original payment method within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

Date Changes & Modifications

Requests to change your reservation dates are subject to availability and may result in a price adjustment. Date changes are not guaranteed and must be approved by Premium Rentals. If the new dates are unavailable or the change cannot be accommodated, your original booking terms remain in effect.

Shortening a reservation (reducing nights) after check-in does not qualify for any refund of unused nights.

Force Majeure & Extenuating Circumstances

In the event of documented government travel restrictions, declared natural disasters, or other extraordinary circumstances beyond the Guest's or Company's reasonable control, Premium Rentals will review cancellation requests on a case-by-case basis. Documentation may be required.

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances including illness, injury, family emergencies, weather events, and travel disruptions. Premium Rentals is not liable for losses caused by events outside our control.

💡 Travel insurance is available through many providers and typically covers cancellation for covered reasons, trip interruption, medical emergencies, and more. We encourage all guests to consider this protection before booking.

Company-Initiated Cancellations

In the rare event that Premium Rentals must cancel a confirmed reservation — due to property damage, emergency maintenance, owner withdrawal, or other unforeseen circumstances — we will:

  • Notify you as soon as possible
  • Issue a full refund of all amounts paid
  • Make reasonable efforts to assist in locating alternative comparable accommodation

Premium Rentals' liability in such cases is limited to the full refund of amounts paid. We are not responsible for any consequential costs such as travel, flights, or other expenses.

No-Shows

If you do not arrive on your check-in date and have not contacted Premium Rentals to cancel or modify your reservation, it will be treated as a no-show. No refund will be issued for no-shows regardless of the applicable cancellation policy tier.
